What is SiteTraQ?

Every single website on the internet has a carbon footprint. Physical hosting servers use electricity that use non-renewable energy sources to keep our internet alive as we know it. Want to know what a website's annual carbon footprint is? Find out by navigating to the "SiteTraQ" tab. Find out Below!

Paste Website Link Here


The websites Carbon Footprint is made up of many factors. This takes in to account a specific page specifications, page views, high data content, site traffic, etc..

**NOTE** It can take to 10 seconds in order to display your results. Please be patient and only click "Submit" once


These calculations are estimates based on monthly site traffic multiplied by the average emissions of a mid-sized website. Real numbers may be slightly higher or lower based on data capabilities of site.

Designed completely in HTML, CSS, and JS. All results are to be taken as near estimates based on Canadian/Ontario averages, and national/provincial data.
